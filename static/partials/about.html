ABOUT

CommonClarity transforms Common Core test data into actionable insights to inform instruction.

The importance of standardized test results in public schools has led many districts to require students to take pretests every few months to prepare. Teachers are inundated with data from these tests and lack the tools and time needed to make sense of it.

That's where CommonClarity comes in.

CommonClarity makes it easy for teachers to upload their data and instantly generate visual reports of their students' strengths and weaknesses, according to the Common Core State Standards. Data are grouped in useful ways to expose patterns and track progress, so that teachers can target instruction and help students improve.
